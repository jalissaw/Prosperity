About Us

Our Mission

Prosperity Enterprise Services was founded by Deon Elmore in 2019. After dedicating nearly 13 years in the Transportation and Trucking Industry I decided that the experiences gathered throughout my journey would be beneficial to helping this industry flourish. That is why PES was founded so that my hard work can make someone else's path easier. Our mission will not stop because I know what can be built with hard work and I know what type of relationships can form under these circumstances. We hope your journey will continue with PES becase we brand our name on our services and we promise always to work hard for you.
